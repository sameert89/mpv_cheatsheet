MPV Cheat Sheet

Playback Controls

SpaceToggle Play/Pause
Left/Right ArrowSeek -5/+5 seconds
Up/Down ArrowSeek -60/+60 seconds
[ / ]Decrease/Increase Playback Speed
mToggle Mute

Volume and Audio

9 / 0Decrease/Increase Volume
Ctrl + [+] / Ctrl + [-]Adjust Audio Delay
ctrl + [+] / ctrl + [-]Adjust Audio Sync
#Switch Audio Track

Subtitles and Video

vToggle Subtitle Visibility
j / JNext/Previous Subtitle Track
z / ZSubtitle Delay (Decrease/Increase)
Shift+g / Shift+fSubtitle Scale (Increase/Decrease)
Alt + [Arrow Keys]Move Video (Pan)
_Switch Video Track
Alt + [+/-]Zoom In/Out

Playlist and Chapters

> / <Next/Previous File in Playlist
PGUP / PGDWNNext/Previous Chapter

Miscellaneous

fToggle Fullscreen
TToggle Stay on Top
qQuit
s / SScreenshot / Screenshot without Subtitles
Ctrl+sScreenshot of Window
